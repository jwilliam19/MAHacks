   Why did we did it / How it works
What if everyday you went to bed not knowing when your next meal was going to be?

What if everyday you went to school wondering if they were going to have food to eat?

These are the many, many problems that people have to face on a daily basis when living in poverty stricken areas.

We wanted to create a way for anybody to be able to contribue to a cause they cared about while at the same time being able to afford and enjoy it.

We are non-profit organization, which means that we make no money from this website. All revenue goes directly to charity.

We generate the money thought ads and vistors on the website itself, and this is how we calcualted how much money someone can make when they play the game.
